## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

Your account is created automatically when you:
1. Complete a Meet & Greet registration, OR
2. Are added by your school's administrator

You'll receive a welcome email with your login credentials.

### Logging In

1. Navigate to `https://app.musicnme.com.au`
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click **Login**

### First Login

On your first login, you should:
- Change your temporary password
- Review your profile information
- Add any missing contact details
- Verify your children's information

### Your Permissions

As a parent, you can:
- ✅ View your children's lesson schedules
- ✅ **Book individual sessions for hybrid lessons**
- ✅ View attendance records and teacher notes
- ✅ Download lesson resources
- ✅ View and pay invoices
- ✅ Book Meet & Greet sessions for new children
- ✅ Update your contact information
- ❌ Modify lesson times (admin only)
- ❌ Add notes to lessons (teachers only)

## Hybrid Lesson Booking

### What Are Hybrid Lessons?

Hybrid lessons are Music 'n Me's unique format that combines:
- **Group weeks** - Your child learns with peers (60 min)
- **Individual weeks** - One-on-one attention (45 min)

This alternating pattern provides the best of both worlds!

### How the Pattern Works

A typical term might look like:
- Week 1: Group session (all students together)
- Week 2: Individual session (book your timeslot)
- Week 3: Group session
- Week 4: Individual session
- And so on...

### Booking Individual Sessions

When booking opens for individual weeks:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Go to **My Bookings** or click the notification
3. Select the child
4. View available timeslots
5. Click on your preferred time
6. Confirm the booking

### Booking Tips

- **Book early** - Popular times fill up fast!
- **Check your calendar** - Make sure the time works for your family
- **Set reminders** - Add to your personal calendar

### Rescheduling a Booking

You can reschedule if needed:

1. Go to **My Bookings**
2. Find the booking you want to change
3. Click **Reschedule**
4. Select a new available time
5. Confirm the change

**Important:** You cannot reschedule within **24 hours** of the session. Plan ahead!

### Cancelling a Booking

1. Go to **My Bookings**
2. Find the booking
3. Click **Cancel**
4. Confirm cancellation

**Note:** Cancellations within 24 hours may not receive credit. Check your school's policy.

### Viewing Your Bookings

1. Go to **My Bookings**
2. View all upcoming individual sessions
3. See booking history

### Booking Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Confirmed** | Your session is booked |
| **Pending** | Awaiting confirmation |
| **Completed** | Session has occurred |
| **Cancelled** | You cancelled the booking |
| **No-Show** | Missed without cancellation |

### Attendance Records

1. Go to child's profile
2. Click **Attendance** tab
3. View attendance by lesson or date range

### Attendance Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ✅ **Present** | Attended the lesson |

| ⏰ **Late** | Arrived late |

View your child's:
- Overall attendance rate
- Attendance by lesson
- Pattern of absences (if any)

### Teacher Notes

Teachers write notes about your child's progress:

1. Go to child's profile
2. Click **Notes** tab
3. View all notes chronologically

### Understanding Notes

Teachers provide notes about:
- What was covered in the lesson
- Your child's progress
- Areas for practice at home
- Achievements and milestones
- Recommendations

### Note Frequency

Teachers aim to add notes:
- After each lesson (ideally)
- At minimum, weekly

If you don't see recent notes, the teacher may be catching up.

## Invoices & Payments

### Viewing Invoices

1. Go to **Invoices** in the navigation
2. View all invoices for your family

### Invoice Information

Each invoice shows:
- Invoice number
- Date issued
- Due date
- Term and period
- Line items (lessons, packages)
- Total amount
- Payment status

### Invoice Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft** | Not yet finalized |
| **Sent** | Awaiting your payment |
| **Partially Paid** | Some payment received |
| **Paid** | Fully paid - thank you! |
| **Overdue** | Past due date |

### Paying Online (Credit Card)

The fastest way to pay:

1. Open the invoice
2. Click **Pay Now**
3. Enter your card details on the secure Stripe page
4. Click **Pay**
5. Receive confirmation email

### Payment Security

- All payments processed securely via Stripe
- Card details never stored on our servers
- SSL encryption protects your information

### Payment Methods Accepted

- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express

### Manual Payment

If you prefer bank transfer or other methods:

1. Contact your school administrator
2. Make payment using provided details
3. Admin will mark invoice as paid

### Payment History

1. Go to **Invoices**
2. Click **Payment History**
3. View all past payments

### Hybrid Lesson Pricing

Hybrid lessons are billed based on the pattern:
- Group weeks at group rate
- Individual weeks at individual rate

Example:
- 6 group weeks @ $25 = $150
- 4 individual weeks @ $45 = $180
- **Total: $330**

## Meet & Greet Booking

### What is a Meet & Greet?

A Meet & Greet is a trial session for new students to:
- Meet the teacher
- Try the instrument
- See if music lessons are right for them

### Booking a Meet & Greet

For a new child in your family:

1. Go to `https://app.musicnme.com.au/book`
2. Fill in child's details
3. Select preferred instrument
4. Choose available date/time
5. Add any notes for the teacher
6. Submit the booking

### What Happens Next

1. **Pending** - Your booking is submitted
2. **Admin Review** - School reviews your request
3. **Approved** - You receive confirmation
4. **Attend** - Bring your child to the session
5. **Enroll** - If satisfied, complete enrollment

### After the Meet & Greet

If you decide to enroll:
1. You'll receive a registration link
2. Complete the registration form
3. Make payment via credit card
4. Child is enrolled in lessons
5. You can then book hybrid sessions

## Account Settings

### Updating Your Profile

1. Click your name in the top right
2. Select **Account Settings**
3. Update your information
4. Click **Save**

### Contact Information

Keep these details current:
- **Primary Contact** - Main parent/guardian
- **Secondary Contact** - Backup parent/guardian
- **Emergency Contact** - For emergencies during lessons

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (twice)
5. Click **Update Password**

**Password Requirements:**
- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter
- One lowercase letter
- One number
- One special character

### Email Notifications

Control which emails you receive:

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **Notifications**
3. Toggle preferences:
   - Lesson reminders
   - Booking confirmations
   - Invoice notifications
   - Teacher notes
   - School announcements

### Deleting Your Account

If you need to delete your account:

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Confirm your decision
4. 30-day grace period begins
5. Account permanently deleted after 30 days

**Note:** You can cancel deletion during the grace period by logging in.

## Tips & Best Practices

### Getting the Most from Lessons

**Encourage Practice**
- Review teacher notes for practice suggestions
- Download resources for home practice
- Set a regular practice schedule

**Stay Engaged**
- Read teacher notes regularly
- Communicate any concerns to teachers
- Celebrate your child's achievements

**Be Punctual**
- Arrive on time for lessons
- Allow extra time for parking/setup
- Notify school of any absence in advance

### Hybrid Lesson Success

**Book Early**
- Set calendar reminders for when booking opens
- Popular times fill quickly
- Have backup times in mind

**Be Flexible**
- Morning slots often have more availability
- Consider different days if your preferred time is full
- Rescheduling is allowed with 24+ hours notice

**Track the Pattern**
- Note which weeks are group vs individual
- Plan family activities around the schedule
- Use the calendar to visualize the term

### Managing Multiple Children

**Use Filters**
- Filter calendar by child
- View resources by child
- Check notes for each child separately

**Separate Bookings**
- Book individual sessions for each child
- Choose times that work for pickups/drop-offs
- Consider back-to-back slots if available

### Payment Tips

**Pay Early**
- Avoid late fees
- Ensure continued enrollment
- Reduce stress

**Check Invoice Details**
- Verify all charges are correct
- Contact admin with questions
- Keep payment receipts

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Can't log in"**
- Check email and password
- Try "Forgot Password" to reset
- Contact support if issue persists

**"Can't see my child's lessons"**
- Ensure child is linked to your account
- Check the correct term is selected
- Refresh the page

**"No available slots for booking"**
- All times may be taken
- Try different weeks
- Contact admin for waitlist

**"Can't reschedule within 24 hours"**
- This is by design to ensure teacher preparation
- In emergencies, contact admin directly

**"Payment failed"**
- Check card details are correct
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Try a different card
- Contact your bank if declined

**"Resources won't download"**
- Check internet connection
- Try a different browser
- File may be temporarily unavailable

**"Notes not showing"**
- Teacher may not have added notes yet
- Check the correct date range
- Contact school if expected

### Getting Help

**For Account Issues:**
- Email: support@musicnme.com.au

**For Lesson Questions:**
- Contact your child's teacher through the portal

**For Billing Questions:**
- Contact your school administrator

**In-App Help:**
- Click the **?** icon for contextual help

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Booking Questions

**Q: How far in advance can I book hybrid sessions?**
A: Booking typically opens 1-2 weeks before the term or when admin opens booking.

**Q: What if I miss the booking window?**
A: Contact your school administrator who may be able to help with remaining slots.

**Q: Can I book the same time every week?**
A: Times are booked per individual week, so you book each session separately.

### Payment Questions

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**
A: Contact your school administrator to discuss payment plan options.

**Q: What if I have billing questions?**
A: Reach out to your school administrator for any billing inquiries.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, all payments are processed securely through Stripe with industry-standard encryption.

### Lesson Questions

**Q: How do I know when my child has group vs individual weeks?**
A: Check the calendar - group weeks show all students, individual weeks show your booked time.

**Q: What if my child is sick?**
A: Contact your school as soon as possible. For hybrid sessions, reschedule if more than 24 hours notice.

**Q: How can I see what was covered in lessons?**
A: Check the Notes section in your child's profile for teacher updates.

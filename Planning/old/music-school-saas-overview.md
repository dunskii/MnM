# Music School Management Platform
## High-Level Overview

## What is it?

A comprehensive cloud-based management platform designed specifically for modern music schools that offer flexible teaching models including individual lessons, group classes, and hybrid instruction. Unlike existing solutions locked into specific regional school terms or limited to 1-on-1 teaching, this platform provides the flexibility and features that contemporary music education requires.

## The Problem We Solve

Current music school management platforms have critical limitations:
- **Rigid teaching models** - focused only on 1-on-1 lessons, ignoring group classes and hybrid approaches
- **Geographic constraints** - locked into USA or European school term structures
- **Clunky scheduling** - difficult to edit bookings, manage multiple locations and rooms
- **Limited flexibility** - can't adapt to different teaching styles and regional requirements

## Key Differentiating Features

### Hybrid Teaching Model
Unlike any other platform, we support true hybrid instruction where:
- Students attend regular group classes (e.g., 4 students, weekly)
- On specific weeks configured by the school (e.g., weeks 4 and 8), group classes pause
- Parents book individual 1-on-1 sessions for those designated weeks
- Schools have complete flexibility to define which weeks are 1-on-1
- System automatically manages the complex scheduling and parent notifications

### Comprehensive File Sharing
Teachers can provide complete learning materials:
- Music scores for structured practice
- Backing tracks for performance practice
- Metronome click tracks for rhythm development
- Example recordings for reference
- Scheduled release ensures students receive materials at the right time in their learning journey

### Global Flexibility
Built for international expansion:
- Schools define their own term/semester structures
- Not locked to any specific country's academic calendar
- Support for regional payment processors beyond Stripe
- Data export compliance with international regulations
- Multi-currency and multi-language ready

### Multi-Tenant Architecture
Each school operates independently with:
- Completely isolated data for security and privacy
- Custom configuration for pricing, terms, and class types
- Branded presence with custom domains and landing pages
- Scalable infrastructure that grows with your school
